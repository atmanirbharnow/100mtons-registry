5. What Happens After Registration?

Our process is simple and transparent, tracking your contribution through milestones:

  1. Register Your Asset: Complete a quick 2-minute registration to list what you have built.
  2. Receive Registry ID: Secure your official, safe tracking profile number.
  3. Asset Classification: Your environmental parameters are categorized.
  4. Development Screening: Your project is reviewed for structural growth paths.
  5. Opportunity Review: Access the platform framework to evaluate future options.
